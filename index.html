Cabinets... Kitchen & Bathroom

This washroom required storage space (it had nothing but a single metal rack before). I made a wall unit for sundries, a cabinet in which the sink is countersunk and taps mounted, hinged door swings open 140 degrees for full access, and on the wall across is an oak 3-hook bath towel hanger. (The house has only one 90 degree corner in that corner of the room where the sink is.)

Furniture & Standing Units

The entertainment center is solid oak, plenty of hidden storage space and display storage space. The unit is 3 separate pieces that can be secured together to act as a single unit, or the towers can be placed in other parts of the room to stand alone. The center (opening between the towers) is 48 inches wide and 25 inches tall off the floor. Each tower is approximately 61 inches tall x 18 inches wide. The curio cabinet is solid oak, 3-sided glass panels for easy viewing and wide front door opens 170 degrees. Both units have all adjustable shelves for modification when altering contents and use. All four pieces (TV unit and curio cabinet) are mounted on casters for smoothness and ease of moving around the room without damaging flooring, and stand firm with no shifting.
